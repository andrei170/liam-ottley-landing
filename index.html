FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to be a developer?

No. Roughly 40% of Accelerator students come from non-technical backgrounds. The curriculum assumes zero coding experience. Modern no-code AI tools handle the heavy lifting.

How long until I'm profitable?

Average timeline to first paid client is 60-90 days. First $10K/mo month is usually month 3-5 depending on how fast you niche down and pitch.

Is AAA still a good opportunity in 2026?

Better than it was in 2023. Demand from businesses is accelerating, but supply of competent builders isn't keeping up. Market is far from saturated.

What if I don't want to start an agency?

The same AAA curriculum works if you want AI skills for your corporate job instead of starting a business. Message the team after joining — we'll point you to the specific curriculum paths.

Who is this NOT for?

People expecting passive income or overnight results. AAA is work. The students who win are the ones who build, pitch, and iterate for 90-180 days.
